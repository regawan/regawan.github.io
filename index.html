Game: Valo

What:
Game submission for Aalto GN! Game Jam #16. A survivor-like game where every shot counts. The world has been consumed by darkness, and your only source of light is the muzzle flash of your trusted rifle. But in the shadows, something lurks—watching, waiting. Just because you can’t see them… doesn’t mean they’re not there.
Why:
To get an end-to-end introduction to the game development process and specifically learn the basics of the open-source game engine Godot.
How:
Made in 38 working hours. Godot 4.4 using GDScript as the programming language and Audacity for sound editing. Used royalty-free asset packs from Itch.io for most assets, as well as Pixabay for royalty-free sounds.
